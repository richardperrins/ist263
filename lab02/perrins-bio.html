Richard Perrins' Biography

Personal Info

My name is Richard Perrins. I am a junior at Syracuse University. I have a dual major in political science in magazine, news & digital journalism, with a specific interest in newspaper reporting and editing.

My main affiliation on campus is with The Daily Orange, for which I have served a variety of roles, including:

  • Asst. Copy Editor
  • Asst. News Editor
  • News Editor
  • Editor-in-chief

Outside of school and work, my main interest is soccer. I am a huge Manchester United fan and have been watching and playing soccer since I was extremely young. I grew up just north of London, England, but now live in Cleveland, Ohio.
